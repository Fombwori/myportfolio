About Faith Kwamboka Ombwori

Faith Kwamboka Ombwori is a Cybersecurity Expert currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity at the University of Essex. She has been widely recognized across Africa as one of the top Women in Technology Leadership, excelling in advancing digital resilience and innovation in cybersecurity. As the head of one of Africa’s most successful cybersecurity firms, Faith has spearheaded projects that safeguard critical infrastructures, financial systems, and government platforms.

Areas of Expertise

  • Cybersecurity Risk Management & Governance
  • Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT)
  • Digital Forensics & Incident Response
  • Secure Systems and Software Development
  • Cybersecurity Awareness & Training Programs

Achievements & Recognitions

  • Named one of Africa’s Top Women in Cybersecurity
  • Keynote Speaker at multiple regional cybersecurity conferences
  • Contributor to national and regional cyber policy frameworks
  • Led enterprise-level penetration testing across banking, healthcare, and government institutions

Vision & Mission

Faith is passionate about empowering the next generation of women in technology while building a secure digital future for Africa. Her mission is to bridge the gap between cybersecurity innovation and practical implementation, ensuring organizations remain resilient against evolving threats.
