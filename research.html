RESEARCH METHODS AND PROFESSIONAL PRACTICE

Research Hypothesis

This research investigates the widespread use of substandard CCTV cameras that fail to meet digital forensic requirements. The hypothesis suggests that such equipment compromises the integrity of forensic investigations by producing unreliable or inadmissible evidence. Findings from this study may highlight the urgent need for reviewing cybersecurity laws, establishing stricter compliance standards, and updating national frameworks to ensure that surveillance technologies meet acceptable forensic quality.
